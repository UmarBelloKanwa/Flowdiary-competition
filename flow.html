Minene dangane da Flowdiary ?

Flowdiary cibiya ce da ta maida hankali akan koyarda digital skills da yaren Hausa domin taimakawa matasa su zamo masu dogara da kansu kuma masu nasara a wannan duniyar fasaha. Munaso mu kirkire kungiyar masu karatu a Flowdiary .Ba kawai dokar karatu ba; ta na akan haduwa da wasu wadanda suke da sha'awa kan kirkire-kirkire na fasahar zamani. Mun samar da dama ga dalibai da suyi huldewa da manyan kwarrarrun ma'aikata a masana'antu, ta hakan za su koya waka junan su sannan kuma su habaka a tare.
